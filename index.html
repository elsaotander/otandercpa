Accounting and Tax Services

Tax Preparation, Payroll Services, Bookkeeping, Training

Tax Preparation

We will prepare individual and small business tax returns. We will implement quarterly tax planning so you will not have a SURPRISE at yearend. We are a paperless office which utilizes a secure client portal and a top-end tax software program.

Payroll Services

We can handle full service payroll or you can enter time and prepare paychecks and we can do the rest-- pay the taxes and prepare the necessary payroll tax returns. We can help with some HR duties to help keep you in compliance with Federal and State requirements.

Bookkeeping

We will do weekly or monthly bookkeeping for your business so your numbers are updated regularly. Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable,Bank Reconciliations, General Ledger, Expense Reimbursements,We can also help organize your paperwork, We can assist with your firm going paperless

Training

I have always loved Teaching and Training- even as a kid. I did some interim training for Idaho Small Business Development Center for Quickbooks. I have done A LOT of one-on-one training for Quickbooks. I actually prefer this way since we can focus on what you NEED.

About

I have over 30 years of experience in Accounting, Tax, Payroll and Bookkeeping services, qualifying me to get the job done effectively and professionally. I received my CPA for the state of New Jersey in 1985, and later received my CPA for the state of Idaho in 2001. In 1997, I earned my CFP (inactive). I have been the owner of Better Bookkeeping since 1988, and have over 30 years of experience working with CPA firms and accounting services. In the past, I have held numerous Treasurer positions on various boards, been a Controller for Atlanta School of Massage, and served as the Director of Accounting for Moxie Java International.
